About REVIVAL FIRE PH

🔥 Experience God's Presence

Revival Fire Philippines is a Christ-centered ministry committed to evangelism, discipleship, healing, revival, and community transformation.


Our vision is to see lives transformed through the power of Jesus Christ and to equip believers to carry revival wherever they go.

Facebook YouTube X (Twitter) Instagram TikTok

🔥 VISION STATEMENT

To see the Philippines and other nations transformed by the Presence and Power of God through a nationwide revival marked by salvation, healing, deliverance, Holy Spirit empowerment, and the manifestation of signs, wonders, and miracles—raising a generation of believers who passionately love and serve Christ the Lord and advance His Kingdom.

✝️ MISSION STATEMENT

Revival Fire Philippines exists to bring revival to the Philippines and other nations by proclaiming the Gospel of Jesus Christ, leading people into a life-changing encounter with God, The Father, The Son And the Holy Spirit, and equipping believers to walk in faith, love, hope, prayer, holiness, consecration and spiritual power.


We are committed to:

  • 1. Preaching the Good News of Salvation and making disciples of Jesus Christ throughout the nation.
  • 2. Promoting Revival and Spiritual Awakening through prayer, worship, evangelism, and the ministry of the Holy Spirit.
  • 3. Ministering Healing and Deliverance to individuals, families, and communities through the power of God.
  • 4. Equipping and Empowering Believers through biblical training, leadership development, and Holy Spirit impartation.
  • 5. Demonstrating God's Love and Power through compassionate outreach, signs, wonders, and miracles that glorify Jesus Christ.
  • 6. Partnering with Churches and Christian Leaders to strengthen the Body of Christ and advance Kingdom purposes throughout the Philippines and beyond.
  • 7. Raising a Generation of Revivalists who will carry the fire of God, influence society with biblical values, and transform their communities for the glory of God.
